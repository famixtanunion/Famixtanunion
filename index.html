Who We Are

About Us

Famixtan Union is a community society founded in 2026, rooted in the vibrant town of Sammanthurai, Sri Lanka.

Driven by a shared love for sports and a deep commitment to social good, our members come together to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those around them. We are a family — bound not by blood, but by purpose.

From community service initiatives to sports events, Famixtan Union stands as a beacon of unity, teamwork, and compassion in our community.
